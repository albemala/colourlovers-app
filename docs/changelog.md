# Changelog

## 2.3.1

- App renamed to **Color Finder: LUV**

## 2.2.0

- The app now asks for a store review at startup (every fifth launch), in addition to after adding favorites
- **Refreshed interface** — restyled "other apps" cards, taller list rows, refreshed pills, and a shared hue-range chip picker across the color filters

## 2.1.0

- **Other apps** promotion now includes Clarity and Textmine; refreshed icons for Exabox, Hexee Pro, and Iro Iro

## 2.0.2

- **Other apps** promotion — replaced the fixed list with a data-driven one and now also appears on the color, palette, pattern, and user detail screens
- About screen polish

## 2.0.1

- Fixed the app failing to load on launch

## 2.0.0

- **Initial release** of the redesigned app
- **Browse** — explore colors, palettes, patterns, and community members, with related items on every detail screen
- **Filters** — narrow lists by sort, hue range (color chips), hex value, creator, and keywords
- **Favorites** — save colors, palettes, and patterns; filter the favorites tab by type and hue range
- **Details** — full color, palette, pattern, and user views, with sharing
- **Share** — share colors, palettes, and patterns to other apps
- **App review prompt** — gently ask for a store review after adding a few favorites
- **About** — credits and links
- **Light and dark themes** that follow the system
